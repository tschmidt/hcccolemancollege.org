HCC Coleman College HITT graduates are eligible to take the Registered Health Information Technician (RHIT) and Certified Coding Associate (CCA) exams sponsored by the American Health Information Management Association (AHIMA) and Certified Professional Coder (CPC) exam sponsored by the American Academy of Professional Coders (AAPC).
Employment Opportunities
Registered Health Information Technicians can find work in hospitals, clinics, insurance companies, physician offices and many other health care related facilities.

HI-Tech Health Program
A health IT professional is someone who is qualified to support the adoption and implementation of Electronic Health Records (EHRs), information exchange across health care providers and public health authorities, and the redesign of work flows within health care settings to gain the quality and efficiency benefits of EHRs.
HCC Coleman College for Health Sciences offers an intensive online CE program training six (6) various Health IT workforce roles and is designed for self motivated experienced Health Care and IT professionals that can be completed in less than six (6) months.
Upon completion of the program, graduates are expected to sit for the national HIT Pro Certification exam developed by the American Health Information Management Association (AHIMA).

Admission Requirements

We are thrilled you want to start your career in health care at HCC Coleman College

However, unlike other HCC colleges, enrolling at HCC Coleman is a competitive and selective process. Some programs require successful completion of courses before you are allowed to apply for admission. Not all programs admit all the applicants they receive. Additionally, some programs accept one class per year, while others admit two.

You can see there are a lot of details involved in choosing the best program for you. That's why we want to get to know you better and ask you to visit our campus in person prior to applying for admission.

But there is on common element to every student's application: you must be a Houston Community College student at the time you apply for admission to an HCC Coleman College health science program. Find the application to HCC at coleman.hccs.edu/about-us/how-to-apply/.

Once you have completed the HCC application, or if you already have an HCC student identification number, visit coleman.hccs.edu to begin your health science application process. If you have questions, contact us at studentsfirst@hccs.edu or chat online with an HCC Coleman representative at askColeman.
