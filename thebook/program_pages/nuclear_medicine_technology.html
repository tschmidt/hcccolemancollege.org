Nuclear Medicine Technology

Degree Type
Associate Degree of Applied Science
Accreditation Agency
Joint Review Committee on Educational Programs in Nuclear Medicine Technology
Certificate Testing
Graduates are eligible to take the Nuclear Medicine Technology Certification (NMTCB) examination and/or the American Registry of Radiologic Technologists (ARRT) exam.
Employment Opportunities
Nuclear Medicine Technologists may find work in hospitals and diagnostic clinics.
Average Salary
Entry-level salaries are in the range between $47,700 and $62,000.
Program Website
coleman.hccs.edu/nmtt

Nuclear Medicine Technologist
  • Eligibility requirements include: passing a TSI state a pproved test and all developmental courses needed for college level English, Algebra, Biology, and Psychology, or transcript(s) with credits in college level Math, Reading and English.
  • The following courses must be completed prior to the second semester of the Nuclear Medicine Technology program: BIOL 2401, BIOL 2402, CHEM 1405, MATH 1314 and SCIT 1420.
 

Admission Requirements

We are thrilled you want to start your career in health care at HCC Coleman College

However, unlike other HCC colleges, enrolling at HCC Coleman is a competitive and selective process. Some programs require successful completion of courses before you are allowed to apply for admission. Not all programs admit all the applicants they receive. Additionally, some programs accept one class per year, while others admit two.

You can see there are a lot of details involved in choosing the best program for you. That's why we want to get to know you better and ask you to visit our campus in person prior to applying for admission.

But there is on common element to every student's application: you must be a Houston Community College student at the time you apply for admission to an HCC Coleman College health science program. Find the application to HCC at hccs.edu/application.

Once you have completed the HCC application, or if you already have an HCC student identification number, visit coleman.hccs.edu to begin your health science application process. If you have questions, contact us at studentsfirst@hccs.edu or chat online with an HCC Coleman representative at askColeman.

Welcome to the first step in your health care career. HCC Coleman College will be with you every step of the way.
