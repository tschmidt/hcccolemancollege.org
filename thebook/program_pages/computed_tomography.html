Program Admission Requirements
Click here to learn more about the basics of applying to an HCC Coleman College program.
Computed Tomography
(For graduate or practicing technologist)
  • Copy of current MRT License from the state of Texas.
  • One letter of recommendation from your immediate supervisor addressing your job performance. Recommendation letter may be sent directly to: Director of Radiography/CT, HCC Coleman College, 1900 Pressler St., Houston, TX 77030 or submitted in a sealed envelope with your application materials.
  • A resume showing dates of education and employment.
  • Official transcripts showing completion of a radiography, nuclear medicine or radiation therapy program.
Computed Tomography
(For current student in a Radiologic Science Program)
  • Official transcripts showing current enrollment in a Radiologic Science program. Upon program completion, submit an official transcript to the Radiography Department as proof of completion.
  • A letter of intent stating who you are, expected date of graduation (proof of completed Registry Application/Receipt from the Registry) that you have applied for the registry or will be applying soon. Any activities related to your professional career should be stated and elaborated on.
  • Only individuals who have passed the registry can be accepted into the program. Copies of ARRT or NMTCB certification must be provided to the program along with a current copy of the MRT License prior to start of program.
  • One letter of recommendation from a faculty member not related to the HCC Coleman College Computed Tomography Program (letter must address your academic performance). Recommendation letter may be sent directly to: Director of Radiography/CT, HCC Coleman College, 1900 Pressler St., Houston, TX 77030 or submitted in a sealed envelope with your application materials.

We are thrilled you want to start your career in health care at HCC Coleman College

However, unlike other HCC colleges, enrolling at HCC Coleman is a competitive and selective process. Some programs require successful completion of courses before you are allowed to apply for admission. Not all programs admit all the applicants they receive. Additionally, some programs accept one class per year, while others admit two.

You can see there are a lot of details involved in choosing the best program for you. That's why we want to get to know you better and ask you to visit our campus in person prior to applying for admission.

But there is on common element to every student's application: you must be a Houston Community College student at the time you apply for admission to an HCC Coleman College health science program. Find the application to HCC at coleman.hccs.edu/about-us/how-to-apply/.

Once you have completed the HCC application, or if you already have an HCC student identification number, visit coleman.hccs.edu to begin your health science application process. If you have questions, contact us at studentsfirst@hccs.edu or chat online with an HCC Coleman representative at askColeman.
