Dental Assisting

Degree Type
Certificate
Accreditation Agency
Commission on Dental Accreditation of the American Dental Association
Licensure Testing
Graduates are eligible to sit for the national certification exam offered by the Dental Assist- ing National Board.
Employment Opportunities
Graduates may find work as a chairside assistant, receptionist, and office manager to the general or specialty dentist in private offices, clinics and other institutions.
Average Salary
Entry-level salaries for Dental Assistants range between $10 and $14 per hour.
Program Website
coleman.hccs.edu/programs/dnta

Admission Requirements

We are thrilled you want to start your career in health care at HCC Coleman College

However, unlike other HCC colleges, enrolling at HCC Coleman is a competitive and selective process. Some programs require successful completion of courses before you are allowed to apply for admission. Not all programs admit all the applicants they receive. Additionally, some programs accept one class per year, while others admit two.

You can see there are a lot of details involved in choosing the best program for you. That's why we want to get to know you better and ask you to visit our campus in person prior to applying for admission.

But there is on common element to every student's application: you must be a Houston Community College student at the time you apply for admission to an HCC Coleman College health science program. Find the application to HCC at coleman.hccs.edu/about-us/how-to-apply/.

Once you have completed the HCC application, or if you already have an HCC student identification number, visit coleman.hccs.edu to begin your health science application process. If you have questions, contact us at studentsfirst@hccs.edu or chat online with an HCC Coleman representative at askColeman.

Welcome to the first step in your health care career. HCC Coleman College will be with you every step of the way.
