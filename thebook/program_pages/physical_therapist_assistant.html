Physical Therapist Assistant

Degree Type
Associate Degree of Applied Science
Accreditation Agency
Commission On Accreditation In Physical Therapy Education
Licensure Testing
Program graduates are eligible to apply for licensure through the Executive Council of Physical Therapy and Occupational Therapy Examiners.
Employment Opportunities
Physical Therapist Assistants may find employment in hospitals, nursing homes, rehabilitation centers, outpatient clinics, school systems and home health care agencies.
Average Salary
Entry-level annual salaries range between $40,000 and $50,000.
Program Website
coleman.hccs.edu/programs/ptha

HCC Coleman recommends that students complete all prerequisite and academic courses before admission to the Physical Therapist Assistant program.

Prerequisites
BIOL 2401 - Anatomy and Physiology I
BIOL 2402 - Anatomy and Physiology II
PSYC 2301 - Introduction to Psychology
Academic Courses
PSYC 2314 - Human Growth and Development: Lifespan Humanities/Fine Arts (3 Credits)
Program Admission Requirements

Physical Therapist Assistant
  • Satisfy TSI requirements, unless exempted.
  • Transcript showing completion of preadmission courses with a minimum grade of C or better.
  • Observation hours documented and verified with PT or PTA signature. (12 hours of inpatient observation and 12 hours of outpatient observation)
  • Satisfy HCC Admissions Requirements for college-level reading and math.
  • Essay on observation hours.
  • Three letters of reference.
  • Signed Essential Functions forms given at Physical Therapist Assistant program breakout session.

Admission Requirements

We are thrilled you want to start your career in health care at HCC Coleman College

However, unlike other HCC colleges, enrolling at HCC Coleman is a competitive and selective process. Some programs require successful completion of courses before you are allowed to apply for admission. Not all programs admit all the applicants they receive. Additionally, some programs accept one class per year, while others admit two.

You can see there are a lot of details involved in choosing the best program for you. That's why we want to get to know you better and ask you to visit our campus in person prior to applying for admission.

But there is on common element to every student's application: you must be a Houston Community College student at the time you apply for admission to an HCC Coleman College health science program. Find the application to HCC at coleman.hccs.edu/about-us/how-to-apply/.

Once you have completed the HCC application, or if you already have an HCC student identification number, visit coleman.hccs.edu to begin your health science application process. If you have questions, contact us at studentsfirst@hccs.edu or chat online with an HCC Coleman representative at askColeman.

Welcome to the first step in your health care career. HCC Coleman College will be with you every step of the way.
