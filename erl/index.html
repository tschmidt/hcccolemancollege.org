The HCC Coleman College Employee Recognition Luncheon is held each year to celebrate the accomplishments of our faculty and staff. While all HCC Coleman College employees are invited to attend, seating is limited and requires an RSVP. Reserve your spot today and join the celebration on May 13th, 2014.

The Awards

Ace Award
This is the highest recognition given to a faculty and a staff member every year. The award is given to the faculty and a staff member who has shown excellence in Accountability, Communications and Ethics and are nominated by the students, faculty and staff of HCC Coleman College.
John B. Coleman Pathfinder Award
This award is given to the individual who has gone above and beyond to distinguish themselves by leading a path for others to follow. They also change and improve the lives of students and shape the future of health care.
Shining Star
This award is given to individuals who have taken the initiative to lead and develop new and innovative projects and ideas.
Exceptional Service Award
Recipients of this award have gone above and beyond in their job responsibilities and exhibit an exceptional work ethic.
