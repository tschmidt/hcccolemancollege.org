Let's Smile!

The HCC Coleman College Dental Hygiene Clinic offers discounted dental treatments and services – teeth cleaning, oral cancer screenings, X-rays and more – to the community. These services are very affordable and insurance is not necessary.

The Cost of the comprehensive oral exam is only $20 for adults and $5 for kids.

Sealants available for just $1.

To schedule an appointment, please call the clinic at 713-718-7292.

HCC Coleman College for Health Sciences
