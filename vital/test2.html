The urgent need for health career personnel in the HCC community and the lack of space for additional students at the HCC Coleman College for Health Sciences leads us to build the institution's infrastructure and capability to address the needs of the student population, HCC proposes Videoconferencing to Improve Teaching and Learning (VITAL).

This five-year grant from the U.S. Department of Education will allow HCC Coleman College in the Texas Medical Center to increase enrollment and graduates to meet the urgent needs of our diverse community. This begins with our "Triage" approach of implementing Voice/Video and Data throughout the HCC community beginning with HCC Coleman College. As our approach to videoconferencing matures, we will seek to implement a strategic process allowing our campuses the opportunity to become even more competitive affording our students and community more opportunity.

V.I.T.A.L also seeks to harness the potential of social media in ways not seen before. We seek to bring direct interaction between student/instructor/educator and medical institutions in direct relationship with their field of study. This combination of voice/video/data collaboration combined with social networking will enable the HCC community and students to better compete in this growing, competitive environment.
