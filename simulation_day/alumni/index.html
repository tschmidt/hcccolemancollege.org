What to Expect

Simulation Day is an intensive day designed to allow HCC Coleman College students to engage, inspire, sharpen and demonstrate their clinical patient care skills through the use of interdisciplinary scenarios.

Experience what it is like to work in a fast-paced health environment as HCC Coleman College transforms into "John B. Coleman Hospital" for one day.

Roles

You can participate as a:

Patient
Serving as a patient will allow students, faculty, and staff to experience health care from the patient's perspective.
Observer
Observe simulation day without actually participating.
