Online Courses

The Center for Healthcare Professionals (CHP) is always looking for ways to bring quality professional healthcare continuing education options to busy healthcare professionals.

The CHP partners with training providers to offer unique and accessible online course offerings. The link below will take you to a listing of online courses currently available that provide approved continuing nursing education (CNE) contact hours through our training partner 360Training. 360Training online course content comes from two sources:

  • Medsenses is an approved CNE provider through the New York Nurses Association which is accredited by the American Nurses Credentialing Center (ANCC)
  • Corexcel is an approved CNE provider directly through the American Nurses Credentialing Center (ANCC)

Click Here to select the CNE online courses that works for you!

The CHP plans on adding more online courses options in the future, so check back with us from time to time to see what’s new.

Note: It is always advisable to consult with your own state’s Board of Nursing to ensure that they will accept sources of approved CNE contact hours that are not pre-approved specifically for your state, for the purpose of your state license renewal. When enrolling for any of these online courses, you are enrolling directly with 360Training (not HCC), and 360Trainingwill provide you with student support services and the certificate of completion with the appropriate CNE verification statement when you complete a course. You will not receive an additional certificate of completion from Houston Community College (HCC), nor will your course completion be recorded on your HCC student transcript.
