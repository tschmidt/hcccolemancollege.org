Course Description:

Nurses participating in this activity will be able to apply the knowledge gained to improve their delivery of appropriate and safe nursing care to the aging patient population, with particular focus on:

Part 1 - The aging demographic and related health challenges, changes in mental status, and special needs for patients with disabilities.

Part 2 - The patient needs related to polypharmacy, oral health, sexuality and end-of life care.

Description:

Community health workers and CHW Instructors are each a part of the changing health care workforce development process. It is important that both CHWs and CHW Instructors participate in this change process through the use of interpersonal and organization core competencies from which to draw knowledge for changes that will affect both practices and policies of the CHW role. A lack of participation will allow others to make important decisions without hearing from community based CHW participants.
