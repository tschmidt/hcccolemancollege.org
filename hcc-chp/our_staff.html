Meet Our Staff

Kirk White, Directory - Kirk White began in May of 2011 as the first Director of the CHP and is a Registered Nurse holding a Master’s of Science in Psychiatric/Mental Health Nursing from the University of Texas at Austin School of Nursing, and a Bachelors of Arts in Environmental Design from Texas A&M University in College Station. Prior to his arrival at HCC, he worked for eleven years in continuing education at Austin Community College. His nursing experience includes working with adolescents and families in a psychiatric in-patient setting, caring for patients in various stages of HIV/AIDS, and teaching and presenting on healthcare and staff/professional development topics. White believes that “every Coleman graduate should be able to come back to Coleman for their professional continuing education, making Coleman a true provider of life-long learning.”

Rosalinda Garza, Program Manager - Rosie has over ten years of experience in higher education, as well as workforce experience within the health care professions. She holds a Bachelor of Science in Health Care Science and a Masters of Physical Therapy from the University of Texas Medical Branch in Galveston. As a Physical Therapist, she has provided in-patient and out-patient care and provided educational trainings to patients and other healthcare providers. Rosie’s experience within higher education includes both instructional and administrative roles within Houston Community College. As a faculty member, she has worked in the classroom as a Healthcare Instructor, English Instructor, and an academic advisor. Rosie’s experience also includes overseeing faculty and developing curriculum for multiple instructional programs, working with Corporate College training programs and monitoring workforce grant budgets and programs.

Blanca Perez, Records Specialist - Blanca Perez is a part-time Records Specialist, and has over six year of experience working for HCC within the Continuing Education department. Her roles have included support responsibilities for various programs, working closely with instructors, students, program managers, and other support staff. Her experience includes managing: student enrollments; course materials and classroom schedules; course sections and other data in PeopleSoft; course files such as rosters, grades and evaluations; and recruiting, registering and assessing students. Blanca holds an Associate Degree in Business Administration from Houston Community College.
