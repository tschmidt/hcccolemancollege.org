CHP Accreditations

The CHP works to ensure that all of its course offerings are approved for professional contact hour continuing education credit by recognized accrediting agencies whenever possible. The CHP is working to expand upon the number of healthcare disciplines it currently provides accredited professional continuing education credit for:

Clinical Laboratory Professionals
HCC Coleman College is approved to award continuing education credit to be used by certificants towards their recertification requirements for the American Society of Clinical Pathology (ASCP) Certification Maintenance Program (CMP).
Community Health Worker
Houston Community College Coleman College for Health Sciences, is approved by the Department of State Health Services (DSHS) for Community Health Worker (CHW) Certification and CEU's.
Dental Professionals
HCC Coleman College for Health Sciences is recognized by the Texas State Board of Dental Examiners (TSBDE) and approved to offer dental continuing education (dental assisting and dental hygiene).
Health Information Technology Professionals
The HCC Coleman College Health Information Technology Program is accredited by the Commission on Accreditation for Health Informatics and Information Management Education (CAHIIM).
Nursing Professionals
The CHP is working to obtain its own Continuing Nursing Education (CNE) Provider Unit status under the Texas Nursing Association (TNA). Currently all course offering for nurses (RN and LVN) are being submitted to the TNA for individual approval for CNE.
Social Work Professionals
Houston Community College is an approved provider of licensed social worker continuing education (CE) under the Texas State Board of Social Work Examiners.

Don't see your healthcare discipline on the list? Some healthcare disciplines do not allow for awarding of a continuing education “providership” to institution such as the HCC CHP. In those cases each course offering is approved individually through the accrediting agency, or it may be incumbent upon the licensed/certified healthcare professional to seek credit for courses completed. Feel free to contact us for more information on your specific discipline’s rules.
